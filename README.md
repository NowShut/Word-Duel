# Privacy Policy — Word Duel

**Effective date: 23 May 2026**

---

## Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Word Duel mobile application (the "App"). It describes our data practices in plain language. The short version: we do not collect, store, process, or share any personal data — not now, not ever.

## Data We Collect

We collect no data whatsoever. The App does not collect, request, transmit, or store any personal information, including but not limited to:

- Your name, email address, or any other contact information
- Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or hardware information
- Location data of any kind
- Usage analytics or behavioural data
- Crash reports or diagnostic data
- Any data entered into or generated within the App

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics services, advertising networks, tracking SDKs, or data brokers. No data is shared with any third party for any purpose.

## Online Multiplayer

Word Duel includes an online multiplayer mode. To facilitate matchmaking and gameplay, the App connects to a game server. This connection transmits only the game state necessary for play (e.g. tile selections and scores). No account is required, no persistent user profile is created, and no information that could identify you personally is sent to or stored on the server.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect data from any users, including children under the age of 13. There is no registration process, no account creation, and no data entry of any personal nature.

## Changes to This Policy

If our data practices ever change, this policy will be updated with a new effective date and made available at the same location. We will never retroactively collect or use data in ways not disclosed at the time of collection.
